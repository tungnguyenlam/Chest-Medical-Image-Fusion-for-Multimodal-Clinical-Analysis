\section{Methodology}
\label{sec:methodology}

In this section, we present the methodology for the clinically-aligned multi-modal chest X-ray classification framework, which we refer to as \textbf{CaMCheX}. We detail the formal problem definition, describe the individual modal encoders (image, text, and vitals), outline the token fusion strategies (comparing the spatially expanded V2 and the collapsed single-token V3 architectures), describe the prior-aware temporal modeling extensions (comparing the concatenated self-attention V3 configuration and the asymmetric cross-attention V4 architecture), and discuss the training objective.
